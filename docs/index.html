My name is Mattis Kindblom and I'm from Sweden. When I was 4 years old when I was brought into the "Gaming World" and when I turned 11 I started taking a look into Competitive FPS games.

My first FPS game that I fell for was CSGO, Counter-Strike Global Offensive which was released 21st of August 2012. I enjoyed that game so much that I kept playing it day in and day out and I ended up stop playing the game after playing it for 12500 hours before quitting. but in that time of playing csgo I found out about streaming, I knew from csgo that you could make a living out of being really good at playing games but when I found out about Twitch I realized that there's no need to become a professional player in a certain game, eventually you'll get bored of that game and then what? The competitive scene won't follow you and feed you. With Twitch you can stream whatever game you want, and if you want to change things up and switch game, just go for it! if you got loyal fans they will follow you. That's what I love about Twitch.

Now I'm here as a Twitch Affiliate, playing games during my free time, having fun streaming 4 times every other week for my small community.

Most people when they get into streaming they thinks it's easy meanwhile it's extremely hard to make a living out of it, others might do it for the clout and others just to have a fun time. I'm streaming because I want to entertain others. I strive to have a professional setup where I can entertain people and not have any issues while streaming and just make an awesome experience for the viewers
